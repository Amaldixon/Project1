Pictute this after a wonderful hot shower you dry off and go to your mirror, just as you wipe off the fog from the mirror you look away and its back and you have to wipe it off again and again and again, so on and so on. 3.zero wrinkles with laundry zero effort

Nothing is more annoying than having creassed clothes especially when it's your favourite ones. Now I know you can just iron them but this is the wonderful joy of this life hack, When I found out about this hack I've never looked back, first what you need to do is get some ice cubes, secondly throw said ice cubes in the dryer. And that's it! Once the ice cubes melt in the dryer the steam will start to do it's magic this will remove all the wrinkles. However this trick isn't as effective with heavier clothes but it will work like a charm with all of your lighter weight clothes.

5.chill wine with frozen grapes

This is one is for all the wine drinks out there, if you want your wine chilled but hate that diluted flavor then this life hack is just for you, and it's so easy simply grab some grapes put them into your freezer till they are nice and frozen. Now try sticking some frozen grapes into your glass instead of ice cubes. p.s. You're welcome.

6.post-it!

Does your keyboard get dirty from time to time? Everytime you open your laptop brushing off bits or blowing away the dust, how about you try using a post-it note. By running the sticky part of the post-it note along your keyboard it will help remove bits of food, pieces of grime, dust, etc. Also post it notes are great for leaving reminders, just stick them on your laptop!!

7.pen spring Protection

Doesn't it get so annoying when you have a charging cable and the ends starts fraying, and not working aswell as it did before which means you have to buy a new one only just to wait for this one to let you down. By adding a pen spring around your cords will keep the ends from fraying. Just what you need to protect all of those phone and laptop cords!

8.easily pour dressings

Instead if removing the foil from dressing or oil, what you should do is poke holes in it instead. That way you'll never have dressing come pouring out of the bottle and getting everywhere saving you time so you don't have to clean up, saving you from over dressing your salads and also from all those extra calories. This works great with vegetable oil, rubbing alcohol and mineral oil.

9.aloe vera ice cubes

Sunburn is a real pain in the butt, and we all know aloe vera makes sunburned skin feel so much better already, but imagine if it were cold. On a hot day after a long time out in the sun a little burnt a bit sore, good thing you came prepeared, start rubbing those soothing aloe vera ice cubes all over the burns. That's the ultimate relief, right there!

10.make your own cosy light

Using this simple life hack to create a cozy light wherever you are. Just switch on your smartphone flashlight and place it under a water bottle. By using your smartphone light underneath a water bottle to make a lantern that distributes light better then the direct beam. The best part of this is you can pick what ever colour you want, whatever the mood is saying that's what you can go for.
